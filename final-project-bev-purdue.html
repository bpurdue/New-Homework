Team Tournament Scorekeeper!

Running a Round Robin Team Tournament Has Never Been Easier!

This unique Round Robin Team Tournament Scorekeeper allows you to run team round robins effortlessly and efficiently by automatically assigning pairings and calculating scores.

What exactly is a round robin team tennis tournament? It is a tournament between two or more teams. Each team brings players, and all of the players on one team play matches against all of the players on the other team over the course of several timed rounds. In each round, the object is to win as many games as possible. At the end of each round, each side reports how many games it won. The team that wins the most games overall wins the tournament.

This Team Tournament Scorekeeper is designed for:

  • Two Teams
  • Singles or Doubles
  • 3 Singles Players or 3 Doubles Partnerships per Team
  • 3 rounds of play
  • 3 tennis courts

The Team Tournament Scorekeeper automatically:

  • Assigns pairings for each round on designated courts
  • Totals games won for each round for each team
  • Calculates the final score for each team and determines tournament winner

The Tournament Director only needs to enter the games won each round! The Tournament Director has the option of effortlessly entering:

  • The names of the two Teams
  • The names of the Singles Players (ex. "Tommy") or Doubles Partnerships (ex. "Bev & Ann")
  • The actual court numbers for the tennis courts being used
